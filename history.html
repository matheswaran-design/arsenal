History of Arsenal FC

Arsenal Football Club was founded in 1886. Over the years, it has won numerous league titles, FA Cups, and other prestigious trophies.

Arsenal has a rich tradition, highlighted by their iconic unbeaten season in 2003-2004, where they finished the Premier League season without losing a single match.
